Project partners

BioSolar Nexus is delivered by a UK–Ukraine consortium bringing together research, engineering, deployment, stakeholder engagement and scale-up capability.

Research lead

Aston University

Aston University leads project coordination, research, technical design and system modelling. Its role includes integration of core subsystems such as PVT, PCM thermal storage, anaerobic digestion and the biogas-fuelled micro-CHP concept, alongside validation and technical knowledge generation.

Integration and commercialisation

Future Cycle Ltd

Future Cycle Ltd leads system-level integration, prototyping and commercialisation activity. The company supports modular design development, technical coordination, fabrication logic, bench testing, documentation and market-facing pathways for future scale-up and deployment.

Field deployment partner

CLIMHOUSE Energy LLC

CLIMHOUSE Energy LLC is responsible for field deployment, installation and commissioning in Ukraine. Its role includes site preparation, system assembly support, calibration, safety validation, operational rollout and practical engineering input for real deployment conditions.

GEDSI and community engagement

National Council for Sustainable Development (NCSD)

NCSD leads stakeholder engagement, social inclusion and GEDSI-related activity. The organisation supports community consultations, impact assessment, inclusive communication materials, policy alignment and wider social learning connected to the project’s implementation.

Collaboration model

What the consortium brings together

The project combines complementary capabilities across the full innovation pathway, from technical design and modelling through deployment, impact assessment and dissemination.

Research & design

System architecture, modelling, validation and technical evidence generation.

Engineering & integration

Prototype logic, modular system integration, testing and scale-up readiness.

Deployment & commissioning

Field installation, practical engineering delivery, safety checks and commissioning.

Social & policy dimension

Stakeholder engagement, GEDSI, community-facing materials and policy relevance.
